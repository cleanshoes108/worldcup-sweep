How points work

Every match one of your teams plays scores points: win = 3, draw = 1, loss = 0 — group stage and knockouts alike, so teams that go deep keep earning. With underdog scoring on, a Dark Horse's points are worth ×1.5 and a Wildcard's ×2 (flip the switch off for flat scoring). Ties break on your teams' combined goal difference, then goals scored.
